About us

TechGizmoHub is an innovative local electronics store with 6 years of experience, a team of 7 staff, and a £500,000 annual turnover, selling products like laptops, smart home devices, gaming consoles, and accessories. The business aims to expand nationwide and engage tech-savvy customers through a professional, user-friendly website, which will be designed, developed, and tested for functionality, accessibility, and a modern, engaging experience.
